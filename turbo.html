Types of Turbo Engines

Here are some of the most common types of turbo engines:

  • Single Turbo
  • Twin Turbo
  • Sequential Turbo
  • VGT Turbo (Variable Geometry Turbo)
  • Electric Turbocharger

Single Turbo

The single turbocharger is the most basic type of turbocharger. It has one turbine and one compressor and is often used in smaller engines.
